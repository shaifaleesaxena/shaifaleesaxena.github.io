## Current research projects

### Robust Deep RL for Time-Varying Control (Particle Accelerator Tuning)
This project studies hybrid learning-and-control methods that combine deterministic actor-critic reinforcement learning with bounded extremum seeking for robustness under drift and nonstationarity. I am also exploring safety-aware supervision to maintain stable behavior when operating conditions move outside the training distribution.

### Multi-Task Reinforcement Learning for Robotic Manipulation
I am training a single policy to solve multiple manipulation tasks, including push, grasp, and pick-and-place, using shared representations and task-conditioned behavior. This work also involves large-scale simulation, ablation studies, and training sweeps to improve generalization across tasks.

### Vision Representation Learning for RL via Autoencoders
This work focuses on building autoencoder pipelines that compress image observations into compact latent states for downstream control and reinforcement learning. The goal is to reduce training time, improve scalability, and make RL more practical for high-dimensional robotic observations.

### Space Robotics: Solar Panel Deployment Failure Detection and Repair
In this project, I work on vision-based failure detection for satellite solar panel deployment and on learning-based corrective control strategies to recover from jammed or stuck mechanisms.

## Prior engineering and research work

### Flight-Control Simulation and EFCS Development
At Airbus, I worked on electronic flight-control system packages for training simulators, malfunction logic, validation workflows, and technical support for control and stability behavior.

### UAV Modeling, Estimation, and Control
At Tata Advanced Systems, I worked on 6-DOF aircraft modeling, autopilot development, Total Energy Control System (TECS) design, extended Kalman filtering, system identification, and flight-performance analysis.

### Swarm Robotics and Autonomous Systems
My earlier engineering work also included swarm algorithms for quadcopters in disaster-relief scenarios, obstacle avoidance, and sensing pipelines using LiDAR and onboard systems.

### Structural Health Monitoring
My undergraduate research studied guided-wave-based online damage detection in aerospace structures using piezoelectric actuators and sensors, along with finite-element modeling and experimental validation.
